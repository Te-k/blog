Privacy is good but it's not enough

11 Jul 2022 · One minute read

I have been working on digital security for more than a decade now, and spent the last 6 years working on digital security for journalists and NGOs, supporting them with digital security, identifying attacks against them etc. That work led me to countless discussions about privacy, privacy issues, privacy scandals and all, but discussing privacy never really included the work I was and am doing. I am helping people at risk to make sure their data are not stolen by government entities. And that is not what we talk about when we talk about privacy.
