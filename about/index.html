About Me

I am Etienne “tek” Maynier, an activist, security analyst and researcher.

I am currently working as a Technologist in the Technology program of Amnesty International on analyzing digital surveillance of Human Rights Defenders. I am also a research fellow at the Citizen Lab.

I am also member of Nothing2Hide and Echap. I was a Mozilla Open Web Fellow in 2016 - 2017.

I enjoy listening and playing music, learning about popular education and political history and theory.
